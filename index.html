Thórsmörk tour

The contrasting vistas of lush oases and roaring glacier rivers cutting through black desert expanses not only make Thórsmörk unique in Iceland but to the entire world. Parts of the valley are rich with moss, fern, and birchwood, while jagged mountain ridges and ice-capped peaks crown the horizon.

Learn more

Hverarönd park tour

Located a short distance from the spectacular volcanic mountain, Krafla, Námaskarð, Hverarönd, is an expanse of hot springs, fumaroles, mud pools and mud pots. Everything is at a boiling point in the stunning arctic desert. No vegetation in sight.

Learn more

the golden circle tour

Spectacular natural sights, such as gushing geysers, roaring waterfalls, and a fissure between two tectonic plates, make this Iceland's most popular sightseeing trail.

Vatnajökull Ice Cave tour

This Ice caving and glacier hiking adventure tour takes place on the mighty Vatnajökull, Europe’s largest glacier. Ice caves are naturally formed and are for that reason ever changing, making them even more charming and unique.

Kayaking on Jökulsarlon Glacier Lagoon is a great way to experience the famous Vatnajökull National Park. Our Jökulsarlon kayak tour will give you a panoramic and unforgettable view of the stunning landscape surrounding the lagoon.
